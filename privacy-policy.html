Privacy Policy

How this site handles customer, analytics, and ad-related data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected on this website and how it is used for order handling, customer communication, analytics, and advertising support.

Last updated: April 20, 2026

By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Customer name, phone number, delivery address, preferred delivery date, and delivery notes submitted during checkout.
  • Cart and order details needed to review requested items and quantities.
  • Basic analytics events such as page views, cart actions, and checkout interactions.
  • Advertising and consent-related data when Google AdSense or related services are active.

How We Use Information

  • To receive, review, and manage order requests.
  • To contact customers about delivery, order clarification, or service support.
  • To understand which pages and actions are useful on the website.
  • To support site monetization and ad compliance where Google AdSense is used.

Local Storage and Order Handling

The current website setup stores some order-related information in the browser's local storage for owner review and dashboard use. This means data may remain on the device or browser used to place or review orders until it is cleared.

If the website later moves to a server-based order system, this policy may be updated to reflect the new storage method.

Cookies, Ads, and Third-Party Services

This site may use Google AdSense, analytics tools, consent technology, and similar services that rely on cookies or related technologies to measure usage and show relevant ads where permitted.

Google and its partners may process data according to their own policies. Users can manage browser settings, cookie preferences, or consent choices where available.
