About Us

Traditional sweets and snacks prepared with everyday care.

Antarmana Sweets & Snacks focuses on familiar Indian taste, fresh batches, thoughtful service, and easy local ordering for families, gifting, and small celebrations.

What We Make

Our storefront highlights traditional sweets and savory snacks like Bura, Bhujia, Sawali, Besan Chakki, Shakkarpara, Petha, Methi Mathri, and Dry Kachori.

We focus on familiar flavors, practical ordering, and products that work well for daily enjoyment, family visits, festive gifting, and small event boxes.

How We Serve

Customers can browse the product list online, choose quantities, submit delivery details, and place an order request using the website checkout flow.

The current website supports Cash on Delivery and QR-based payment confirmation to keep ordering straightforward and accessible.

Freshness First

We aim to keep preparation simple and consistent: small-batch handling, direct customer contact, and clear order review before dispatch.

The website is designed to make it easier for customers to reach us, understand pricing, and send accurate order details without confusion.
