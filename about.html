About Us

A local mithai and snacks storefront built around familiar taste.

Antarmana Sweets & Snacks focuses on traditional Indian sweets, crunchy namkeen, direct owner communication, and a simple online order flow for families, gifting, and everyday cravings.

View Products Contact Us

What We Make

Our menu currently includes Bhujia, Sawali, Besan Chakki, Shakkarpara, Petha, and Khastha Kachori.

The focus stays on a compact signature list that feels familiar, festive, and practical for repeat orders and gifting boxes.

How Orders Work

Customers can build the cart online, add delivery details, choose Cash on Delivery or QR payment, and then send the order summary on WhatsApp.

This keeps ordering simple while still allowing direct follow-up with the owner for confirmation and delivery coordination.

Why Customers Choose It

The website is designed to feel cleaner than a plain price list while still staying easy to use on mobile and desktop.

Clear pricing, short product descriptions, and direct phone support help reduce confusion for first-time customers.
